Tables

Player
Cloneable that handles relationships between Clients and Mobs.

Fields:

  • id: Our unique ID.
  • state: Current state. Always a member of the PlayerState table.
  • client: The Client we are associated with.
  • mob: The Mob we are associated with.
  • messageMode: Our message mode. Always a member of the MessageMode table.
